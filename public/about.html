About Buzz's Lighthouse

Historic Coastal Dining in Naples, Florida Since 1980

A Family-Owned Naples Landmark Offering Classic Florida Flavors and Unforgettable Coastal Views.

The Beginning of a Dream

Our story began in the mid-1970s when Henry “Buzz” Dugan and his wife Judy discovered the tranquil beauty of Naples and Vanderbilt Beach. Captivated by the area, they found a perfect piece of waterfront property in 1978 and started building their dream.

Opening Our Doors to Naples

In March 1980, Buzz's Lighthouse Restaurant officially opened. Buzz and Judy aimed to create a place where families could gather, enjoy warm weather, sip on cold drinks, and savor food fresh from the Gulf.

A Naples Classic Through the Years

Despite the rapid changes in the area and challenges like Hurricane Ian, our commitment to excellent food and heartfelt service has never wavered. Buzz's Lighthouse continues to thrive as a family-run business.

A Celebration of Tradition and Family

Every meal at Buzz's Lighthouse is a celebration of our rich history and culinary traditions. We invite you to join us and create lasting memories with your loved ones.
