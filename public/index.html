Our Journey

Welcome to Buzz's Lighthouse Restaurant, a beloved staple of Vanderbilt Beach since 1980. Throughout the years, we've seen many changes around us, but our commitment to quality and hospitality has never wavered. We pride ourselves on serving fresh seafood alongside a fantastic selection of tropical cocktails, liquor, beer, and wine. Both locals and visitors enjoy our delicious cuisine and stunning waterfront views. With both indoor and outdoor dining options, there’s a perfect spot for everyone.
